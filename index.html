N.D. Convent

School has 1.5 acre land fully utilized for study and sports. School is situated near town with lush & Green atmosphere. it is 500 meter from its nearest railway station. Amethi and Police station at equal distance . The School is established in the year 2011 with strength of 40 students and 3 teachers , with continuous efforts of skilled and qualified teachers and staff school has progressed and achieved it successful journey with current strength of 400+ students . School has vast playground for Football, Hockey , Basketball, Badminton, Volleyball etc both for Girls and Boys .
